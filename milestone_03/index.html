ABOUT VALORANT

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical FPS made by Riot Games. Two teams fight to attack or defend bomb sites using guns and unique Agent abilities. Good aim, clear vision, and fast reactions are very important. Your in-game settings, like sensitivity, resolution, and crosshair, can greatly change how the game feels and how well you play. This website helps you understand and optimize those settings for Valorant.
