A radar or spider or web chart is a two-dimensional chart type designed to plot one or more series of values over multiple quantitative variables. So this is very suitable for my data. Because my data has many variables. It can help users better understand my data and make my data more accurate. I can also understand whether my Hypothesis are accurate. — Spider chart (D3 Graph Gallery)
