sensitivity & FPS

About:Sensitivity & FPS are the two biggest levers that shape how aiming feels. Sensitivity controls how far your crosshair moves per mouse input (eDPI = DPI × sens), while FPS affects motion clarity and input latency. This site helps you find a balanced setup: test and compare sensitivities, calculate eDPI, see how different FPS caps feel, and learn practical tips for consistency. The goal isn’t the “perfect number,” but a stable setup you can repeat, train with, and trust in matches.
