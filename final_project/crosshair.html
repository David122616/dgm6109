crosshair

About:A crosshair is your on-screen aiming reference. Its design affects how fast you acquire targets and how precisely you place shots. Thin lines and a small gap favor precision; thicker lines and a bigger gap improve visibility at a glance. High-contrast colors help on busy maps, while static (no error) crosshairs build consistency; dynamic options that react to movement/firing can teach timing but add visual noise. The goal isn’t a “pro’s exact code,” but a shape you can see instantly, repeat across matches, and train into muscle memory—under your resolution, UI scale, sensitivity, and FPS.
