PC-peripheral

About: PC peripherals are a big part of your VALORANT performance. A good gaming mouse with the right DPI and polling rate helps you aim more precisely. A comfortable keyboard and mousepad make your movement and flicks smoother. A high-refresh-rate monitor (144 Hz or higher) makes enemies look clearer and easier to track. A good headset helps you hear footsteps, skills, and direction more accurately. In this guide, we focus on how to choose and set up your mouse, keyboard, monitor, and headset so that your hardware supports your play style instead of holding you back.
